FAQ

Yes. Alerts can be toggled per channel so you receive only what you need.

Is there a daily digest?

Yes. After the market closes, you get a summary of movers and alert health.

How do I review past sessions?

Session exports capture alerts, settings, and AI audit logs for review.

They’re included with Investor Pro so you can simulate strategies and export reports.
